About

I’m a multidisciplinary designer with a strong foundation in branding, UI/UX, and communication. With a passion for meaningful design, I craft digital experiences that are both engaging and strategic.

I’m Sanaz Habibi, a designer with over six years of experience in graphic design, UI/UX, and marketing communications. My work focuses on building clear, user-friendly designs that connect with people—whether through intuitive digital interfaces, strong brand identities, or impactful campaign visuals. I currently work at EMCO Corporation, where I manage internal and external communications and improve web usability across platforms. At Pulse InfoFrame, I contribute to product design by creating wireframes, design systems, and user flows that support real-world healthcare solutions.
