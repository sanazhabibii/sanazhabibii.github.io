Fermaksan – Fertile Soil, Strong Future

This project involved designing a compelling visual campaign to promote industrial feed mixers and agricultural machinery for Fermaksan. The campaign focused on empowering farmers through clear, bold messaging and modern visuals that evoke trust, productivity, and resilience. Deliverables included:

  • Bilingual Social Media Graphics (Turkish & English)
  • Product Feature Ads highlighting machinery capabilities
  • Farmer Testimonials & Storytelling to build trust and relatability
  • 3D Image Compositing to blend real machinery with farm environments
  • Typography Hierarchy to emphasize slogans and technical specs
  • "Strong herds, strong future." – Messaging was crafted to emotionally connect with farmers while driving sales for high-tech equipment.
